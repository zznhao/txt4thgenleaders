The Dream Chapter Eternity

The Dream Chapter: Eternity is TXT's second extended play, released on 18th May 2020. It contains six songs with the title track "Can't You See Me?"

Tracklist

  1. Drama
  2. Can't You See Me?
  3. Fairy of SHampoo
  4. Maze in the Mirror
  5. Puma
  6. Eternally

Achievements

  • Win on The Show
  • Win on Show Champion
  • Peaked at 1st place in Japanese Albums Oricon Chart
