The Dream Chapter: Star

The Dream Chapter: Star is TXT's debut extended play, released on 4th March 2019. It contains five songs with the title track, "CROWN".

Thw group name TOMORROW X TOGETHER means the five boys come together under one dream in hope of building a better tomorrow.

Tracklist

  1. Blue Orangeade
  2. CROWN
  3. Our Summer
  4. Cat & Dog
  5. Nap of a star

Achievements

  • First music show performance on Mnet's M Countdown
  • First music show win on SBS MTV's The Show with "CROWN"
  • Wins on M Countdown and Show Champion
  • Peaked at 1st place in South Korea Alums Gaon chart and US World Albums Billboard chart
